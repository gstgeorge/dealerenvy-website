As an automotive photographer at Dealer Solutions you'll get great pay, flexible hours that work with your schedule, and an independent working environment. We're always accepting applications from dependable, detail-oriented, candidates.

No experience with photography is required, training and equipment are provided.

Responsibilities

  • Visit assigned dealership(s) regularly to photograph vehicles
  • Communicate with store management to determine a list of priorities or special requests
  • Follow up with direct supervisor daily to discuss any open/closed tasks
  • Upload daily workload into Dealer Solutions dashboards to promptly reflect on dealership websites and 3rd party websites; CarGurus, AutoTrader, Cars.com, etc.

Qualifications

  • Self-motivated and able to work independently
  • Reliable vehicle to commute to dealers
  • Clean driving record to operate vehicles during photo process
  • Full-time daytime availability
  • Comfortable using phones, cameras and computers
  • Ability to work outdoors in all temperatures
  • High-speed internet connection
