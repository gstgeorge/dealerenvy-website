Interested in joining our team?

As an automotive photographer at Dealer Solutions you'll get great pay, flexible hours that accommodate your schedule, and an independent working environment. We're always accepting applications from dependable, detail-oriented, candidates.

No experience with photography is required, training and equipment are provided.

Responsibilities

  • Visit assigned dealership(s) regularly to photograph vehicles
  • Communicate with store management to determine a list of priorities or special requests
  • Follow up with supervisor daily to discuss any open/closed tasks
  • Upload daily workload into Dealer Solutions dashboards to reflect on dealership and 3rd party websites; CarGurus, AutoTrader, Cars.com, etc.

Requirements

  • Self-motivated and able to work independently
  • Reliable vehicle to commute to dealers
  • Clean driving record
  • Full-time daytime availability
  • Comfortable using phones, cameras and computers
  • Ability to work outdoors in all temperatures
  • High-speed internet connection
