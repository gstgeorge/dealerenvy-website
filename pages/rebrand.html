Dealer Solutions is now Dealer Envy

On January 1, 2025 Dealer Solutions Corp has rebranded as Dealer Envy Inc.

While our name has changed, our core values, services, and commitments to our clients remain unchanged. Our rebranding has not affected our day-to-day operations, and we will continue to provide the same high-quality service you have come to expect from us.

A notice was sent to all clients containing our new mailing address and W-9. If you did not receive this information, or have any questions concerning this change, please don't hesitate to contact us.
